WELCOME TO ANGULAR 1 vs ANGULAR 2

Today were going look at two poplular javascript frameworks by building a small todo list app two different ways to compare differences between them.

Both frameworks are/were backed by google and still popular to this day

The Goal:

What we hope to convey


  • Understand general concepts of Angular 1 and 2
  • Get a sense of Angular 1 and 2 syntax
  • Have you decide if either of these frameworks are right for you, or your projects

All the finished code, starter projects, and notes are available at: https://github.com/CurtisKreidler/angular-workshop

Setup

						~$ git clone https://github.com/CurtisKreidler/angular-workshop
~$ npm install
~$ npm start
