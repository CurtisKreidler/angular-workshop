Terminology

Data-binding: Angular 1 works by binding data to your html markup changes that are made in the markup are bond to your model via a $scope object

Controllers: Manipulate the data in your View/Model

Directives: Angular allows you to write your own HTML tags that you can reuse throughout your application (similar to Angular 2 components)
